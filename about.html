Origin Story

We would like to take this opportunity to welcome you to your home to great tasting Thai dishes. We serve only the freshest and finest ingredients. The original founder of this establishment came up with the idea of opening a Thai restaurant when one day he noticed the scarcity of Thai restaurants in this Jersey City, despite the large population of Thai people it inhabits.
